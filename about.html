Our Story

About Feast Fortune

Fifteen years of culinary passion, craftsmanship, and heart

How It Began

Born from a Passion
for Excellence

Feast Fortune was founded in 2010 by Executive Chef James Harlow with a singular vision: to bring restaurant-quality cuisine and world-class hospitality directly to people's most important moments.

What began as intimate private dinner parties in a single city has grown into a full-service luxury catering company, having served over 500 events across three continents — from intimate engagements to 1,000-guest galas.

Our name carries a promise: every feast we create becomes your fortune — a priceless memory etched in flavor, elegance, and joy.

"Food is not just nourishment. It's the centerpiece of every meaningful human gathering."

— James Harlow, Founder & Executive Chef
